## Foreign income and FTC

- Map each reviewed `foreign_sources[]` item to Schedule FSI using its country, income head, gross income, foreign tax, Indian tax, and relief section.
- Map relief totals to Schedule TR and Part B-TTI.
- Section 90 relief must not exceed the reviewed DTAA tax limit in addition to the foreign-tax and Indian-tax lower-of checks.
- File Form 67 within the applicable deadline before relying on claimed relief. A pending status remains visible as a warning.
- Schedule FA is not generated by this assembler. Upload or enter the source skill's separately reviewed FA A2/A3 data.
