# Schedule selection tick-list (utility "Select Schedule" step)

After picking ITR-2, the utility pre-ticks the mandatory schedules and lets you add/remove optional ones.
Produce a tick-list mapped to the taxpayer's actual income. Full mapping table in
[../references/schedule-mapping.md](../references/schedule-mapping.md#which-schedules-to-select-in-the-utility).

Common misses:
- **FSI + TR** unticked while claiming FTC (both needed or the FTC won't flow — not auto-ticked).
- **112A** left ticked with no 112A income.
- **ESOP** ticked for ordinary MNC RSUs (it's only for deferred tax on eligible start-up ESOPs, s.80-IAC).
- **80D / VI-A** ticked under the NEW regime, where only 80CCD(2) employer-NPS survives.
- **HP** ticked with no house-property income.
- **AL** only if total income > ₹1 Cr.
